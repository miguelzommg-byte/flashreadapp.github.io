Support Center & FAQ

How do I start reading?

Tap Library at the bottom of the screen, then tap the + button to import a document (PDF, EPUB, DOCX, or plain text). Once imported, tap any book to open it and press the play button to begin RSVP reading.

What is RSVP reading?

Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP) shows one word at a time in a fixed position so your eyes never move. This eliminates the main bottleneck in reading speed and lets many people read 2–3× faster with strong comprehension.

How do I change my reading speed?

Use the − and + buttons in the Reader to lower or raise your words-per-minute (WPM). Your speed is saved automatically. Free users can read up to 300 WPM; Premium unlocks unlimited speed.

What's included in Premium?

  • Unlimited reading speed (300 WPM+)
  • PDF, EPUB, and DOCX file support
  • Full personal library with unlimited books

Premium is available as a Monthly ($4.99), Yearly ($19.99), or Lifetime ($49.99) purchase.

How do I restore a purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID you used to purchase. Restoration is instant if your subscription is active.

My app crashed or isn't working

Try the following steps:

  • Force-quit the app and reopen it
  • Make sure your iPhone or iPad is running the latest iOS version
  • Delete and reinstall FlashRead from the App Store
  • If the problem persists, email us — we respond within 24 hours

How do I delete a book from my library?

Swipe left on any book in your Library to reveal the Delete button, then tap it to remove it.
